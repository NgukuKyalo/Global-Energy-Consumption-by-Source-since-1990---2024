## Abouth the project
This projects acts as a global energy consumption overview. Using Our World in Data (https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/global-energy-substitution) "Global Primary Energy Consumption" Dataset, with the goal of identifying trends on energy consumption over the years.
The analysis moves from raw data cleaning to deep exploratory data analysis.

...
## Objectives Covered

1. What is overall trend of global primary energy consumption since 1800?
2. What are the recent changes in renewables energy consumption since 1990? Which renewable energy is utilized the most?
3. What is the growth trend in recent years for the renewable energy consumption comapared to 1990 as the baseline?
4. What is the global primary energy consumption mix in 2024?
5. What is the comparison between fossil fuels and renewable energy primary energy consumption since 1990 - 2024?
